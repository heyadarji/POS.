Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our payment solutions and services

What payment methods do your systems support?

Our payment systems support all major credit and debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), mobile payments (Apple Pay, Google Pay, Samsung Pay), contactless payments, and various digital wallets. We also offer ACH processing for direct bank transfers and can integrate with cryptocurrency payment options upon request.

How secure are your payment solutions?

Security is our top priority. All our payment solutions are PCI DSS compliant and utilize end-to-end encryption to protect sensitive data. We implement tokenization technology to ensure card details are never stored on your systems. Our fraud detection algorithms continuously monitor transactions for suspicious activity, and we regularly undergo security audits to maintain the highest standards of protection.

What are your processing fees?

Our processing fees vary based on your business type, transaction volume, and the specific solution you choose. We offer transparent pricing with no hidden fees, and we're committed to providing competitive rates. Contact our sales team for a customized quote tailored to your business needs. We can often beat competitor pricing while providing superior service and technology.

Do you offer integration with existing business software?

Yes, we offer seamless integration with a wide range of business software including popular POS systems, e-commerce platforms, accounting software, CRM systems, and inventory management tools. Our API is well-documented and designed for easy implementation. Our technical team can assist with custom integrations for proprietary or specialized systems.

What kind of customer support do you provide?

We provide 24/7 customer support through multiple channels including phone, email, and live chat. Our dedicated support team includes technical specialists who can quickly resolve any issues that may arise. We also offer comprehensive documentation, video tutorials, and regular training sessions to help you make the most of our solutions.
